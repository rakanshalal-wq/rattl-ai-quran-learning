# Product Vision

Rattl is being designed as a focused Quran memorization experience for children and parents.

The goal is not to replace teachers or traditional learning environments. The goal is to support daily practice, make progress easier to follow, and reduce the friction parents face when trying to keep children consistent.

## Core Problem

Many families want their children to memorize Quran consistently, but daily follow-up is difficult.

Common challenges include:

- Children need a simple and calm experience.
- Parents need clear progress visibility.
- Practice sessions need structure.
- Audio and repetition matter.
- The experience must respect Arabic and Quran learning contexts.

## Product Direction

Rattl focuses on three product principles:

1. **Simple for children** — the session should not feel complex or crowded.
2. **Clear for parents** — progress should be visible without technical language.
3. **Careful with AI** — AI should support the learning flow, not become the center of the experience.

## Public Repository Scope

This public repository is only a showcase. It does not include the private MVP source code, backend, database schema, payment logic, AI evaluation logic, or production infrastructure.
